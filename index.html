Key Coordinates

Dumas Beach: 21°05'24"N, 72°45'36"E
Tapi River Mouth: 21°06'12"N, 72°44'24"E
Red Soil Sites: Interior areas 15-30km east of Surat city

Required Permissions

  • Surat Municipal Corporation for beach access and sampling
  • Gujarat Coastal Zone Management Authority for sediment collection
  • Sports Authority of India for volleyball court sand specifications

Analytical Methods

  • Grain Size: Sieve analysis (4φ to -2φ at 0.5φ intervals)
  • Mineralogy: XRD analysis, heavy mineral separation
  • Geochemistry: ICP-MS for trace elements, iron oxide quantification
